An inter-governmental forum for 21 member economies in the Pacific Rim that promotes free trade throughout the Asia-Pacific region.

GOALS OF APEC

APEC (Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation) aims to promote economic growth, cooperation, trade, and investment across the Asia-Pacific region. Its primary goals include:
Promoting free trade
Promoting economic coorporation
Promoting sustainable development

Promoting Trade and Investment Liberalization

Reduce trade barriers such as trriffs and quotas
Among member economies to facilitate smoother
More efficient trade and Investment flow

Encouraging Economic Cooperation

APEC fosters collaboration on various issues
To help member economies achieve shared prosperity
