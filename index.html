Goals Of APEC

APEC (Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation) aims to promote economic growth, cooperation, trade, and investment across the Asia-Pacific region. Its primary goals include:
Promoting free trade
Promoting economic coorporation
Promoting sustainable development

Promoting Trade and Investment Liberalization

Reduce trade barriers such as trriffs and quotas
Among member economies to facilitate smoother
More efficient trade and Investment flow
Convenient for goods, services, investment, and people to move across borders.

Encouraging Economic Cooperation

APEC fosters collaboration on various issues
To help member economies achieve shared prosperity
This allows members within the group thrive in multiple economic sectors.

Supporting Sustainable Development

APEC promotes policies that encourage economic growth
Addressing challenges such as climate change and resource management

Strengthening Regional Integration

APEC aims to enhance regional economic integration, which includes harmonizing standards and regulations to improve trade efficiency
Improve supply chain connectivity
Building institutional Capacity

Capacity Building and Technical Assistance

Promote Innovation and Digital Economy
Recognizing the importance of digitalization, APEC supports policies that foster innovation, improve digital infrastructure, and encourage economic participation in the digital economy.
